Bio

 

My name is Mouminatou Barry and I am a web/app developer and a graphic designer. That afternoon I met with Pete Danielsen, EVP of Programming, and he inspired me to explore opportunities within BET.
My research through BET Careers led me to a position within creative, social and digital productivity.
You'll find my experience includes coding, digital marketing and graphic design which has built me into the creative individual that I am today. Ideally, I'd want to express my talents through mediums that play a role in society.
My long term career goal is to create apps and products that would bring the community together and educate our youth. Technology should connect us socially, but ironically it has made the majority of new generations self-centered and I want to reverse that effect. Visiting the BET office, I found great synergy within the work environment and I envision myself working with teams full of creative minds.
